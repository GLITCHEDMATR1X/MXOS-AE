About This Project

A prototype hub designed to grow

GLITCHED MATRIX Prototype Lab is positioned as a digital lab where games, browser experiments, tools, workflows, and system modules can live inside one branded environment.

The goal is not just a single title page. It is the front end of a growing platform for testing, modding, creation, publishing, and play across multiple kinds of experiences.

Lore

The world behind the lab

The brand gains more identity when it feels like a surviving system: damaged infrastructure, preserved prototypes, and unfinished experiments being pushed back online.

That fiction helps the lab, the tools, and the future services all feel like part of the same world instead of separate products.
