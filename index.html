About the Project

Matrix OS: Arcade Evolution is built as a playable digital environment where games, prototypes, tools, and experimental systems can live inside one evolving platform.

The site should feel like the operating surface of that world: industrial, dark, minimal, and glowing with restrained teal neon.

Status Active Development
Theme Black / Grey / Teal
